Diva.js is a web-based document viewer optimized for high resolution image collections.

Built for easy integration

By design, Diva.js integrates easily with existing digital library systems and websites.

Optimized for large images

Diva.js dynamically loads content only as it is needed, meaning that it can serve gigantic, high resolution views of documents with extremely fast load times for the user.

Grid view

Get an overview that preserves the integrity of your document's structure.

Inline image manipulation tools

Engage with your documents via realtime browser-based controls of rotation, contrast and brightness, and zoom level. More coming soon.

Easily share page views

Share the part of a page you're viewing with a click of the link button, so you can send your colleagues objects of interest on the document.

Flexible plugin infrastructure

Easily extend Diva.js by writing your own plugins. Plugins exist for easy downloading, image manipulation, and highlighting of documents. Using our API and Events system, it's easy to write your own.

Mobile-compatible

Designed with mobile document viewing in mind. Tap, pinch-zoom, and swipe through your documents.
